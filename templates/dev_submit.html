Plugin Information
Submission Guidelines
  • Your repository must be publicly accessible
  • Include a valid manifest.json at the root
  • Tag your releases (e.g. v1.0.0)
  • Your plugin must extend AbstractPlugin
  • No dangerous PHP functions (exec, eval, etc.)
  • Prefix your DB tables with your plugin identifier
Review Process
1
Submission

Your repo is cloned and automatically scanned for security issues.

2
Manual Review

Our team reviews the code, manifest, and security scan results.

3
Publishing

Approved plugins are packaged, signed with RSA, and listed on the marketplace.
